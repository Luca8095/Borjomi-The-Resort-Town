Why this website?

1. Challenge

Borjomi is a unique tourist destination known for its natural resources, healthy climate and waters, and cultural heritage. However, this tourist destination, as a resort, does not have a flexible and modern website that provides foreign tourists and Georgian vacationers, Borjomi residents, and local businesses with the necessary information. This leads to a decrease in tourist flow and economic opportunities.

2. Auditor

Foreign tourists and local vacationers - looking for information about the Borjomi Gorge, climate, sights, cultural heritage sites, hotels, and food establishments. Treatment seekers - guests who want to be treated with Borjomi mineral waters, spa and relaxation procedures and are looking for such health centers. Business representatives - hotels, restaurants and travel service agencies who are interested in placing advertisements on the website in order to attract more tourists. Local population - those who offer various services to the guests of the gorge, be it family hotels or food establishments.

3. Insight

My website will be a “Borjomi Digital Guide”, featuring the following features: map, local attractions and cultural heritage sites, hotels, cafes-restaurants, internal transport, activities and planned tours. The website will have a travel plan generator that will help guests plan individual and group routes. Blog - with articles about health, rafting, mountain skiing and eco-travel.
