Borjomi Central Park

  • 4.6

Borjomi Central Park, established in 1850, is a prominent attraction in Borjomi, Georgia, renowned for its natural beauty and mineral springs. The park spans over 35 hectares, featuring diverse flora, amusement rides, and the famous Ekaterina Springâ€”the original source of Borjomi's mineral water.

The park also offers family-friendly activities, including playgrounds, a Ferris wheel, and an adventure zone with zip lines and rope courses. For those interested in history, the nearby Borjomi Museum of Local History provides insights into the town's past and the development of its famed mineral waters.

Overall, Borjomi Central Park serves as a serene retreat, combining natural beauty, wellness opportunities, and engaging activities for visitors of all ages.
